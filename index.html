1 In short

SehatCare is an offline-first health app. All health information you enter — symptoms, vitals, medicine reminders, family profiles, mental-health screening scores, menstrual cycle logs, diary entries, and chat conversations with the on-device AI Doctor — stays on your phone.

We do not run a server that receives your health data. We cannot see your data.

There are no accounts. Sign-in is a local PIN set on your device. Nothing about your identity leaves the phone. The app does make network requests in three specific, user-initiated situations described in §3.

3 Network activity — what and why

SehatCare uses the internet only for these operations, all triggered by you:

  1. AI model download. The Gemma 4 language model (E2B or E4B variant, ~2.5–3.6 GB) is downloaded from a source you explicitly pick — HuggingFace (huggingface.co) or Google Drive. No health data is sent with the request. The download server sees only your phone's public IP address, User-Agent, and the fact that a Gemma file was requested.
  2. Government scheme lookup ("From webpage"). If you tap From webpage and provide a URL, SehatCare fetches that URL's HTML for one-time on-device parsing by the AI. The URL is one you typed; only that URL is fetched. Your health data is not sent.
  3. Find Nearby Doctor. If you tap this button, SehatCare opens Google Maps with a search query built from the suggested specialist name. Google Maps then follows its own privacy policy.
SehatCare does not send analytics, telemetry, crash reports, or advertising identifiers anywhere.

4 AI processing — where it happens

The AI Doctor (Gemma 4) runs entirely on your device after the one-time model download. Every message you type into the chat, every symptom you enter into Symptom Checker, every question fed to the AI for pattern-spotting or briefing generation is processed locally.

Nothing is uploaded to any AI service.

5 Accounts

SehatCare does not use any cloud account system. Sign-in is a local PIN set on first launch, with optional biometric unlock. No email, no password, no Google Sign-In. The PIN and your display name never leave your device.

7 Children

SehatCare is intended for users 13 years and older. It is not designed for children under 13 and should not be used to make medical decisions for infants without a doctor's supervision.

8 Data retention and deletion

Because SehatCare stores everything locally, deleting the app deletes your data. To delete data while keeping the app installed:

  • Individual features have "Clear" / "Delete" controls (Reports, Chat sessions, Medicine reminders, Diary, etc.).
  • To wipe everything the app has stored: Android Settings → Apps → SehatCare → Storage & cache → Clear storage. This resets the PIN, deletes health data, and removes the AI model file.

We have no server-side data, so there is no server-side deletion request to make.

10 Third-party services

SehatCare intentionally minimises third parties. The following are contacted only in the specific scenarios described in §3:

HuggingFace (huggingface.co) — model host you can pick when downloading Gemma. HuggingFace Privacy Notice
Google Drive (drive.google.com) — alternative model host you can pick. Google Privacy Policy
Google Maps (maps.google.com) — used only if you tap Find Nearby Doctor. Google Privacy Policy

The AI Doctor is Google's Gemma 4 (E2B / E4B) open-weights model, run on-device. No inference request reaches Google or any AI provider.

14 Health data — detailed disclosure

a. What health data SehatCare collects

SehatCare collects the following categories of health-related data, entered manually by you:

  • Vitals — blood pressure readings, blood sugar levels, body weight, body temperature, and their timestamps.
  • Symptoms — free-text symptom descriptions you type into the Symptom Checker or Symptom Diary, and the AI's suggested guidance in response.
  • Medicines — medicine names, strengths, dosage instructions, and reminder schedules you create.
  • Prescription scan output — medicine names and frequencies extracted from photos of prescription labels that you scan.
  • Mental wellness check-ins — PHQ-9 and GAD-7 aggregate scores, wellness band, timestamp, and a safety flag. Individual questionnaire answers are discarded immediately after scoring and are never stored.
  • Menstrual cycle log — cycle start and end dates, flow level, associated symptoms, and notes you enter.
  • Vaccine record — dates of vaccines you mark as given and any custom vaccine entries you add.
  • Drug interaction reference — medicine pairs you look up; the query and result are processed and displayed locally only.
  • AI chat history — the full text of your conversations with the on-device AI Health Assistant, stored locally so you can review past sessions.

b. Purpose of health data collection

Each category of health data is collected solely to provide the feature you explicitly use:

No health data is used for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond the feature that collected it.

c. Processing location

All health data is processed entirely on your device. SehatCare does not transmit any health data to a server, cloud service, or any third party. The AI Health Assistant (Gemma 4) runs locally after a one-time model download — no health data leaves your device during AI inference.

d. Health Connect

SehatCare does not connect to, read from, or write to Android Health Connect or Google Fit. No health data is exchanged with any platform health data store.

e. User consent and control

You provide health data voluntarily by using specific features. You may:

  • Skip any feature entirely — the app functions without any health data entered.
  • Delete individual entries within each feature using its built-in Clear / Delete controls.
  • Wipe all health data at once via Android Settings → Apps → SehatCare → Storage & cache → Clear storage.

By entering health data into any feature, you consent to it being stored locally on your device for the purpose described above.

f. No third-party sharing

SehatCare does not share, sell, license, or transmit health data to any third party, advertiser, analytics provider, or AI service. The three network operations described in §3 do not involve any health data.

g. Security of health data

  • Stored in the app's private directory (/data/data/com.example.sehatapp/), inaccessible to other apps.
  • ADB backup is disabled (allowBackup="false"), preventing backup to Google servers or a PC.
  • All network activity uses HTTPS; cleartext HTTP is blocked by the network security config.

h. Data retention

Health data is retained on your device until you delete it. There is no automatic expiry. Since SehatCare has no server, there is no server-side retention period and no server-side deletion to request.

11 Medical disclaimer

SehatCare is an informational aid only. It does not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. The AI Doctor's outputs are model-generated and may be wrong. Always consult a qualified doctor for medical decisions. In an emergency, call your local emergency number.
